# 🚗 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The Road Accident Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze road accident trends, casualties, vehicle involvement, weather conditions, road surface conditions, and accident locations. The dashboard helps stakeholders identify high-risk areas and understand the factors contributing to road accidents for improved road safety and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall accident and casualty statistics.
- Compare current year performance with previous year.
- Analyze accident severity levels.
- Identify vehicle categories involved in accidents.
- Examine the impact of weather and road surface conditions.
- Discover accident hotspots through geographical mapping.
- Support data-driven road safety initiatives.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Slight casualties account for the majority of road accident casualties.
- Most accidents occur on dry road surfaces.
- Single carriageways experience the highest number of casualties.
- Cars are involved in the largest share of accidents.
- Certain geographical regions exhibit significantly higher accident density.
